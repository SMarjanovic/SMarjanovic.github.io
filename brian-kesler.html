About the Board
The Prairie Fields HOA is governed by a Board of Directors (BOD), per the HOA BYLAWS. The Board exists to represent and support all members of the homeowners association. Each member of the Board is elected to a two year term, with an election held in the fall of each year for the vacancies of the following year.

Board Meetings
The BOD generally meets at 8:00 PM on the second Monday of each month in the library at Carrie Busey School. All email items are discussed at the regular monthly board meeting, so that the meeting minutes reflect emailed action.
